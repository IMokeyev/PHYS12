HandBot

Project Concept:

A safe, accurate, stablized, remote control robotic arm that allows the user to do precise work from afar, whether it be from a few meters away due to dangerous materials, or from miles away to perform the work remotely. This arm would use camera hand tracking technology froom the MediaPipe library in order to capture the movements and position of the user's hand, then send it over a wireless signal, and translate it into motion of the arm, wrist, and phalanges through space.
